Format of mail

Subject: Inquiry About [Destination] Trip

Dear [Your Name],

I hope this email finds you well. My name is [Customer's Name], and I am writing to inquire about planning a trip to [Destination]. After hearing wonderful things about this destination, I am eager to explore its beauty.

I would appreciate it if you could provide me with some information regarding the following:

1. **Destination**: I am interested in visiting [Destination] for my upcoming trip. Can you please provide details about the must-see attractions and activities in the area?

2. **Budget**: I have a budget of approximately $[Your Budget] for this trip. Could you recommend travel packages or accommodations that align with my budget?

3. **Duration**: I am planning to stay for about [Number of Days] days. What itinerary would you suggest to make the most of my stay?

4. **Number of Persons**: I will be traveling with [Number of Persons] people. Are there group rates or family-friendly options available for accommodations and activities?

I am looking forward to your recommendations and guidance in planning this trip. Please feel free to contact me via email at [Your Email] or phone at [Your Phone Number] to discuss further details.

Warm regards,

[Customer's Name]
